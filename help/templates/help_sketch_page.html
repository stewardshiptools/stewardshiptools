Heritage Records
This heritage records page shows a table of records above and a slippy map below. The map and the list are linked - filters applied in the table above determine what records are displayed on the map. The map updates automatically based on changes to the table.
Click VIEW in the table will take you to the individual record.
Click on a feature in map will open a pop-up with a link to the feature.
If there are 10 records in the table, only 10 records will display on the map.
Filter Results: The table/map can be filtered by entering terms in this field. All the fields in the table, for all the Species records, are filterable. Fields that are not in the table are not available. Example: __pot______ displays 17 results in table and map. Note that ‘pot’ also returns ‘spot’. __pot_agri_____ displays 2 results. Note: the filter does not take AND or OR strings, nor “”. SETTINGS: The structure of the table can be adjusted by opening the SETTINGS button and modifying the table configuration. You can remove fields from the table by clicking on the radio buttons beside field labels. You can also change the number of rows in the table to 5, 25, 50 or 100. Example: entering __deer__ into the Filter field and selecting 100 from SETTINGS results in a table with 75 records in the table and 75 features in the map. Map controls: Zoom and pan using your mouse. You can also zoom with the “+” and “-” controls, top left. Clicking on the Full screen button will expand the map tot he full size of your web browser window. Change the map base map on the top right by clicking the different layer radio buttons. {# ################################################################################## #}
