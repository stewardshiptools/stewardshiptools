This page lists all the information for one Heritage Cultural record.

The page is divided into:
  • Title bar - The record's type and name
  • Appearances - Where the record occurs in the Interview transcripts and an excerpt of that part of the interview
  • MTK Cultural Fields - Cultural-specific data collected during GIS digitizing
  • Map (right) - The record's spatial feature (point, line or polygon) shown on an interactive map
  • MTK Record Fields - General records data collected during GIS digitizing


Map Notes
  • Zoom - click the +/- buttons at the top-left of the map OR use your scroll wheel OR pin gesture on a tablet or touch screen
  • Pan - drag you cursor or finger/stylus on a touch screen
  • Show in Full Screen - click to see the map expand to show in your full browser window
  • Base Map - Use the radio buttons in the Layer Legend, top-right of map, to choose a base map like Open Street Map (OSM) Mapquest or Mapquest Satellite
  • Overlay Layer(s) - turn on/off the overlay layers using the check mark buttons below the Basemap layers. Overlay layers may have a Pencil button and a Garbage-can button: use these to edit or delete the overlay layer.
